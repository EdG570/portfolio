How it all started

Hi, my name is Ed Glazenski. I have been a registered respiratory therapist for the last ten years but have always had a burning to desire to learn front end development. At the start of 2015 I decided to make that committment and haven't looked back since.


After about six months of learning on my own I decided to take the next step which was taking the UX/UI design course at Bloc. From there I enrolled in Thinkful's front end developer course.


Where my passion lies?

Although I fully understand the UX/UI design process, I have a stronger affinity for front end development and want to continue to learn, grow and become the best front end developer I can.


Just some fun facts

My hobbies include bass guitar, sports and fishing. I have a pet bearded dragon named Miyagi. Oh, I'm also known to be an avid Seattle Seahawks, San Antonio Spurs and Chicago WhiteSox fan.
